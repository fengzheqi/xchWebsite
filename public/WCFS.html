Background

WeChat Pay Flagship Store (WPFS) is a deep cooperation between WeChat Pay and various industry brands, including GAP, Shell, McDonald's, KFC, Rainbow, etc. The purpose of WPFS is to promote no cash life and expand off-line usage of Wechat Pay.

Crazy Cart was proposed to warm the atmosphere of Rainbow WeChat Pay Flagship Store. In Rainbow WPFS, once users pay via WeChat Pay, they will become members of Rainbow automatically. And the electric membership card will be collected in their WeChat Cards & Offers. I was responsible for designing Crazy Cart.





Design Challenge

- Research

Rainbow Market on-site study;
Staffs interviews;
WeChat Pay promotion activities analysis;

After interviewing the manager of Rainbow supermarket, I found what they want mostly was the increase of membership number and purchase per capita.

As in WeChat Pay marketing activities held before, users only noticed they can use WeChat Pay when they pay the bill. How to guide users to perceive WeChat Pay before shopping? Because WeChat Pay is not only a method to pay the bill.


The design challenge was how to design the Crazy Cart which can bring value to both Rainbow and WeChat Pay.

- Game Rules

Crazy Cart was easy to play. Users just needed to scan QR code which was put on each shopping cart in Rainbow Supermarket, then the corresponding results would appear.

There were 3 results:
• Saved 50 RMB when payed the bill
• Saved 5 RMB when payed the bill
• Products within the shopping cart were all free, up to 1000 RMB
