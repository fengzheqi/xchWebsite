Background

WeChat is the most popular instant messaging app in China. Combined with WeChat official accounts, WeChat Pay service explores O2O and off-line shopping experience. With WeChat Pay enabled on mobile phone, users can make transaction payment at anytime and anywhere, suchas in ordering taxi, supermarkets, hospitals and gas station. WeChat Developer Training Class(WDTC) is one of the methods to expand WeChat Pay around the China. However, this WDTC had been evaluated as inefficient and my job was to figure out why it was inefficient and explored solutions to optimize it.



Working Flow



Project Research

- Key Roles Map

Through related staffs and participants interviews and key roles analysis. I found the design challenge was:

How to redesign the WDTC to satisfy all the different needs of various key roles?

Key Roles Map



- Breakthrough Point

I found there was a key goal behind WDTC, and WDTC itself was one of the solutions for the key goal. But WDTC cannot achieve the key goal, because of“low conversion rate”. If we can increase conversion rate, the design challenge could be overcome.




Problem Define

- Service Blueprint

Through analysis of WDTC’s service blueprint, 5 potential problems were found, which may lead to low conversion rate.

Effect Analysis

- Questionnair analysis

The result of questionnair provided useful data for the preparation of the next WDTC.


- off-line case exhibition analysis

The average number of case PDF downloading (users can scan the QR code to download the WeChat Pay cases) is around 130 for each case board.

For each case, averagely 70 persons joined in a WeChat study group by scaning the QR code to communicate with case owner. Even though case owners were not at present, they can deliver information to participants and find proper cooperators. The efficiency of delivering information was improved.
